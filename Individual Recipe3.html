Wambatu Moju

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggplants (aubergines), cut into strips or cubes
  • 2 medium-sized onions, thinly sliced
  • 3-4 green chilies, sliced (adjust to your spice preference)
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • ½ teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2-3 tablespoons vinegar (white or apple cider vinegar)
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste)
  • Salt to taste
  • Cooking oil
    		•

    Steps to Wambatu Moju

    • Begin by preparing the eggplant. Cut the eggplants into strips or cubes and soak them in lightly salted water for about 10-15 minutes. This helps to reduce bitterness. Then, drain and pat them dry with a paper towel.
    • Heat some oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the eggplant pieces and fry them until they turn golden brown. Remove the fried eggplant from the pan and set it aside on a paper towel to remove excess oil.
    • In the same pan, add a little more oil if needed. Add mustard seeds and fenugreek seeds. Let them splutter and release their aroma.
    • Add thinly sliced onions to the pan and sauté until they turn golden brown.
    • Stir in the sliced green chilies and cook for a couple of minutes.
    • Add turmeric powder and mix well with the onions and chilies.
  • Now, add the fried eggplant to the pan and gently mix it with the onion mixture.
  • Pour in vinegar, sugar, and salt according to your taste preferences. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.
  • Reduce the heat to low, cover the pan, and let the Wambatu Moju simmer for about 10-15 minutes. This allows the flavors to blend and the eggplant to absorb the tangy-sweet sauce.
  • Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  • Once the eggplant is tender and the flavors are well infused, remove from heat.
  • Let it cool down to room temperature before serving. Wambatu Moju can be stored in a clean, airtight container in the refrigerator for a few days, allowing the flavors to develop further.
  • Enjoy your homemade Sri Lankan Wambatu Moju as a flavorful side dish with rice or any main meal!
