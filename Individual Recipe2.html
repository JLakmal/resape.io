Fish Ambul Thiyal

Ingredients

  • 1.500g firm, fleshy fish, such as tuna, seer fish, or red snapper
  • 10 goraka fruits
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, minced
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon roasted black peppercorns
  • 1/4 teaspoon curry leaves
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried chilies
  • 1/4 cup coconut milk
  • Salt to taste

Steps to Making Fish Ambul Thiyal

  • In a bowl, combine the fish with the goraka fruits, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and roasted black peppercorns. Marinate for at least 30 minutes, or up to overnight.
  • Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion and sauté until translucent.
  • Add the garlic and ginger, and sauté for another minute until fragrant.
  • Add the curry leaves, dried chilies, and coconut milk. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat to low and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the fish is cooked through.
  • Season with salt to taste
  • Serve hot with rice, string hoppers, or your favorite flatbread
