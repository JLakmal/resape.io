Polos Curry

Ingredients

  • 1 kg green jackfruit, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup thick coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1-inch ginger, minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 curry leaves
  • 1-inch ginger, minced
  • 1 pandan leaf (optional)
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/4 tsp coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Steps to Making Polos Curry

  • Heat oil in a large pan or pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent.
  • Add the garlic and ginger, and sauté for another minute until fragrant.
  • Add the curry leaves and pandan leaf (if using) and fry for a few seconds.
  • Add the turmeric, chili powder, cumin, coriander, and cardamom powders. Mix well and toast the spices for a minute until fragrant.
  • our in the coconut milk and water. Stir well and bring to a simmer.
  • Add the jackfruit pieces and salt to taste. Cover and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the jackfruit is tender.

Serve the Polos Curry hot with steamed rice, roti, or any other accompaniment of your choice.
